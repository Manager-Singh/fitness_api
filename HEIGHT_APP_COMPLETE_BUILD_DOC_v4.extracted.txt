The completion % is a SINGLE shared number across Lifestyle + Habits.

The math (use the CORRECT habits cap of 12 — see Part 3):
total_possible = lifestyle_max_points + habits_max_points
earned         = lifestyle_earned + habits_earned
completion_pct = (earned / total_possible) * 100

Only when habits are also maxed (12) does it hit 41/41 = 100%.

Adult Nutrition Redesign


Applies to ADULT accounts only. (Teen nutrition unchanged.) Replaces the old 13-food logging system with two clean, measurable sections. Almost no typing.

2.1 Points
Protein: 1 pt per 10 g. Cap 9. Caps out at 90 g.
Hydration: Water 1 pt / 500 ml · Spine drink 2 pts / 500 ml. Cap 6. Caps out at 3 spine drinks (or 3 L water).
Total nutrition cap: 15.

protein_points   = min(9, floor(total_protein_grams / 10))
hydration_points = min(6, (water_500ml x 1) + (spine_drink_500ml x 2))
nutrition_points = protein_points + hydration_points   # max 15
Routes to Engine 1 exactly as adult nutrition does today. Spine drinks are worth double on purpose — do not equalize.

2.2 Section A — Protein
UI: one horizontal progress bar (Protein 40 / 90 g · +4 pts) + quick-add chips (one tap = add grams, no math):
Chicken breast +30 g · Steak +26 g
2 Eggs +12 g · Beans/lentils +18 g
Greek yogurt +17 g · Tofu +17 g
Whey scoop +24 g · + Custom (manual grams)
Salmon +25 g
Each tap advances the bar + points; an undo (minus) removes the last add. Bar caps at 90 g / 9 pts (extra grams log but don't add points past 9).
Copy (subtitle): Protein rebuilds the back, core, and glute muscles that hold your spine tall. Log what you eat through the day — aim for 90 g.

2.3 Section B — Hydration (Spinal)
UI: one points meter (0-6), two log methods — Water (row of 500 ml bottle icons, +1 each) and Spine Drinks ('+ Add spine drink' → 6-item picker, +2 each).
The six spine drinks (all support discs/connective tissue): Bone Broth (collagen+glycine, highest value), Watermelon Juice (water+electrolytes), Coconut Water (electrolytes), Cucumber Juice (water+silica), Celery Juice (hydration+anti-inflammatory), Beet Juice (circulation+nutrients). Any combination counts.
Copy (subtitle): Your spinal discs are nearly 80% water and slowly compress as they lose fluid through the day — it's part of why you're shorter at night. Refill them daily. Water counts — and spine drinks like bone broth, watermelon, or celery juice do more for your discs and connective tissue, so they're worth double. · Water +1 pt / 500 ml · Spine drinks +2 pts / 500 ml · 6 pts a day.

2.4 Why spine drinks are worth double (for the team)
Water gets ~90% of the spine benefit (rehydration). Spine drinks add electrolytes (water stays in the discs), collagen+glycine (broth — the building blocks of discs), and silica/anti-inflammatory compounds. So 500 ml of broth/watermelon does more than 500 ml of water — the 2:1 ratio encodes that.

2.5 Data model
Per-day adult nutrition log: protein_grams (int), water_ml (int), spine_drinks (array {type, ml} or count of 500 ml units + type). Derived/served: protein_points, hydration_points, nutrition_points (server-authoritative — never trust a client total). Retire the 13-food list + chocolate.

2.6 Acceptance tests

2. 3 spine drinks → 6; 6 water → 6; 1 spine + 2 water → 4; 4 spine → 6 (capped).
3. Nutrition never exceeds 15.
