COBRA STRETCH — 30 sec hold × 2 sets

GLUTE BRIDGES — 15 reps × 3 sets

CAT-COW STRETCH — 10 reps × 2 sets

HAMSTRING STRETCH — 30 sec each leg × 2 sets

DOORWAY CHEST STRETCH — 30 sec each side × 2 sets

#3. Switch sides

CHILD'S POSE WITH ARM WALKS — 30 sec × 2 sets

SPINAL TWIST STRETCH — 30 sec each side × 2 sets

BUTTERFLY STRETCH — 30 sec × 2 sets

CHIN TUCKS — 12 reps × 2 sets

SUPERMAN HOLD — 20–30 sec × 2 sets

PLANK — 30–60 sec × 2 sets

#3. Engage your core — don't let your hips sag

JUMP ROPE — 60 sec × 2–3 sets
#1. Hold the rope handles at hip height (or pretend if no rope)
#2. Jump with both feet, keeping jumps low and quick
#3. Land softly on the balls of your feet

BODYWEIGHT SQUATS — 20 reps × 3 sets
#1. Stand with feet shoulder-width apart
#2. Lower your hips back and down like sitting in a chair
#3. Stand back up — keep your chest tall

BOX JUMPS — 10 reps × 2 sets
#1. Stand in front of a sturdy box or step
#2. Bend slightly and jump explosively onto it
#3. Step down (don't jump down) and reset

HIGH KNEES — 30 sec × 2 sets
#1. Stand tall, run in place lifting knees toward your chest
#2. Pump your arms — stay light on your feet
#3. Aim for waist-height knees each rep

MOUNTAIN CLIMBERS — 30 sec × 2 sets

DEEP SQUAT HOLD — 30 sec × 2 sets

#2. Place your elbows inside your knees and gently push them apart

LUNGES — 12 reps each leg × 2 sets
